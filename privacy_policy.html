Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

GLOWUP BEAUTY AI ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Camera & Photos

The App uses your device camera to perform AI-powered skin analysis, aging simulation, and before/after photo comparisons. Photos captured through the App are:

  • Processed locally on your device.
  • Not uploaded to any external server.
  • Not shared with any third party.
  • Stored temporarily in your device's cache and can be deleted at any time by clearing app data.

b) Location Data

The App may request approximate location access to provide weather-based skincare recommendations. Location data is:

  • Used only to fetch local weather information.
  • Not stored or transmitted beyond the weather API request.

c) Local Storage

The App stores skin diary entries, streak data, cycle tracking data, routine preferences, and scan history locally on your device using SQLite and SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs that track users.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • OpenWeatherMap API — to fetch weather data for skincare recommendations. Their privacy policy: openweathermap.org/privacy-policy
  • Razorpay — for processing subscription payments. Payment information is handled entirely by Razorpay and is not stored by the App. Their privacy policy: razorpay.com/privacy
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security measures. We do not maintain any external databases or servers that store user data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
